Garlic Shrimps

Garlic shrimp is a delectable seafood dish celebrated for its simplicity and bold flavors. Succulent shrimp are saut√©ed to perfection in a rich garlic-infused butter or olive oil, creating a savory and aromatic experience. The dish's irresistible combination of plump shrimp and fragrant garlic makes it a beloved culinary delight enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon seafood seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, or to taste (optional)
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges, or as needed (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons butter in a cast iron skillet. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.

  2. Toss shrimp with seafood seasoning and place in the skillet. Cook shrimp until pink and opaque, 2 to 3 minutes on each side.

  3. Add 1 tablespoon butter and remove from heat. Garnish with chopped parsley and lemon wedges.
